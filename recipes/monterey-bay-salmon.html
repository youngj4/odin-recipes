Montery Bay Smoked Salmon

Description:

Salty, umami smoked salmon, grat on bagels with cream cheese

Ingredients:

  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 2 cups soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons vinegar
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • A few drops of Tabasco sauce
  • Salmon Filets

Steps

  1. Combine marinade ingredients and mix thoroughly. Set portion aside for basting
  2. Marinate the fish overnight in the refrigerator.
  3. Set grill at about 180°.” Smoke the salmon for about 5-8 hours until you like the texture. You can sprinkle the marinade over the fish while it cooks for added flavor and aroma while it smokes.
